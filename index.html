Company Description

Alpha Cellular, situated on Cowey Rd, Essenwood, Durban, is an accredited in-warranty Nokia Mobile Care and Microsoft Device Care Repair Center specializing in mobile smartphone, tablet, and laptop repairs.

We also specialize in out-of-warranty repairs to all other makes and models of handsets and tablets, viz., Apple, Samsung, Huawei, Honor, Hisense, Oppo, Vivo, Xiaomi, Redmi, Sony Xperia, LG, Mobicel, Mecer, Lenovo, Sansui, Telefunken, Alcatel, Macbooks etc.

Established in 2002, Alpha Cellular has since been servicing end-user clients, corporates, franchise stores (Vodacom, MTN, Cell C, Virgin Mobile, Telkom, Nashua, Autopage, Edgars, Truworths, Foschini, Woolworths, Markhams, JET, CNA, Total Sports, Sports Scene, etc.), as well as independently owned cellular stores in the KZN region.

Course Objective

Due to the high unemployment rates worldwide and the need for skills development, our smartphone repair course aims to provide a work readiness, guided hands-on training and certification to individuals seeking a career in smartphone/tablet repairs. We empower prospective individuals with the necessary skills to troubleshoot and successfully repair common faults on the following makes of devices: Apple, Samsung, Nokia, Huawei, Honor, Redmi, Xiaomi, OPPO, Vivo, Hisense, Mobicel, etc., thereby enabling them to be self-sustaining.

With the increasing demand for skilled technicians, our course will equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in this rapidly growing industry.

What does the course cover?

Our smartphone/tablet repair course teaches you the following skills and techniques.

  • Level 1 and 2 repairs: removing and replacing malfunctioning mechanical and electrical parts (desoldering and soldering).
  • Identification of smartphones and tablets.
  • Identification and usage of tools, equipment, and spare parts.
  • Disassemble smartphones and tablets.
  • Troubleshooting and fault finding (hardware/software), identification of liquid damage.
  • Liquid damage repair techniques.
  • Software fundamentals.
  • Assemble smartphones and tablets.
  • Purchasing/supply of high-quality spare parts.
  • Quality Control.

Return on investment

Typical repair examples of common screen replacements:

iPhone X screen replacement retail price: approx. R650.00 less the cost of the new replacement screen module R350.00 = R300.00 profit.

Samsung A04 screen replacement retail price: approx. R450.00 less the cost of the new replacement screen module R200.00 = R250.00 profit.

Approx. 3 to 4 successful repairs daily = approx. R1000 profit.

Evaluation

A theory and practical assessment will be conducted after the training course to ensure that the individual is capable of successfully repairing a smartphone and tablet. Thereafter, all students will receive a certificate of completion upon successful completion of the course.
